LEGAL AND REGULATORY INFORMATION
================================

OWNERSHIP LAWS:
- Freehold ownership available for foreigners in designated areas
- Registration through Dubai Land Department (DLD)
- Property registration fee: 4% of purchase price
- No property tax or annual wealth tax

RENTAL REGULATIONS:
- Standard rental contracts (Ejari registration)
- Rental increases regulated by RERA
- Security deposit typically 5% of annual rent
- 90-day notice for rental termination
